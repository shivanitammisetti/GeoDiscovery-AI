About GeoDiscovery

A role-based NASA Earth science data platform that connects students and researchers to satellite datasets, live disaster events, and AI-powered recommendations.

What GeoDiscovery Does
🌍
Live Disaster Globe
Real-time 3D visualization of NASA EONET events — wildfires, floods, volcanoes and more on an interactive globe.
📊
Dataset Explorer
Browse 12+ curated NASA satellite datasets filtered by your role. Students get beginner datasets, scientists get advanced ones.
🤖
AI Recommendations
Content-based recommendation engine suggests related datasets based on disaster type and shared tags.
📚
Research Blogs
Expert articles explaining how satellite remote sensing works for disaster monitoring — from MODIS to SAR.
🔍
Smart Search
Search-as-you-type suggestions powered by backend API. Filter datasets by event type, difficulty level, or sort order.
💬
Community Feedback
Users can leave reviews and ratings. New visitors can read community feedback before exploring datasets.

How It Works
1. Select your role
Choose Student or Scientist. Your role determines which datasets you see — students get beginner to intermediate, scientists get everything including advanced SAR and multispectral data.
2. Explore the live globe
The 3D globe shows real-time NASA EONET disaster events as colored markers. Click any marker to see event details and related datasets.
3. Discover datasets
Search by keyword, filter by disaster type, sort by difficulty or name. Each dataset card shows satellite, resolution, use cases, and links to the official NASA portal.
4. Get AI recommendations
Our content-based recommender scores datasets by shared disaster type and tags, surfacing the most relevant ones for your research.
5. Save and revisit
Save datasets to your profile for quick access. Leave feedback to help other researchers find the best datasets for their work.
